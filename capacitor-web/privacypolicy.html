Pace Privacy Policy

Pace uses health data only when you choose to connect Android Health Connect. The app can read steps and weight so your nutrition plan and progress view stay up to date.

Health data

Health Connect access is optional. Pace asks for read access to steps and weight. This data is shown in the app and, if you are signed in, saved to your Pace account so your devices stay in sync.

Control

You can remove access at any time in Android Health Connect settings. If access is removed, Pace will stop importing health data.
