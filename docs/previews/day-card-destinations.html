Salmon & greens

Pan-seared salmon, charred broccolini, lemon-tahini drizzle.

540
kcal
42g
protein
18 min
total

Ingredients · serves 1

  • Salmon fillet160 g
  • Broccolini1 bunch
  • Tahini1 tbsp
  • Lemon½
  • Olive oil1 tsp

Steps

  1. Heat pan medium-high. Salt the salmon, skin-side down 4 min.
  2. Flip 2 min. Rest off heat.
  3. Char broccolini in same pan, 3 min, splash of water.
  4. Whisk tahini + lemon + 2 tsp water. Drizzle, plate, eat.
