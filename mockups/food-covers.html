Recommendation: Style B (serif monogram) is the cheapest to ship and reads the most premium — the italic letter pairs with the Fraunces body type already in the app, and it scales gracefully whether the recipe name is short or long. Photography (Style D) is the strongest visually but requires a curated image per recipe; we'd need a fallback for new ones.

Hybrid worth trying: Editorial wash (A) as the base + a tiny Iconcraft mark (G) in the corner for slot recognition. Best of both — calm, magazine-like, but still scannable.
