Pace · food page redesign

Three directions for the food page

"Your foods" leaves the page entirely. Diet preference is set in onboarding; ingredient dislikes are flagged inline from a recipe or week view via "Don't suggest", then editable from You → Settings → Foods to skip. The food page becomes a single act: generate the week. Each day is the hero.

Option A — Today is the hero

A big "today's plate" card up top. The other six days live as a horizontal ribbon. Shopping list is a sticky pill that pops up from the bottom. Day detail = recipes + steps in-line.

Entry points

Three ways in, one screen

  • · 🛍️ button in the week header (top-right)
  • · "Need anything?" tile inside any day
  • · Swipe up from the bottom of the week

Why a full screen

It's a destination, not a side panel

A real shop is a 20-minute job. A bottom-sheet feels temporary; a full screen lets you check items off without dismissing context every time. Back arrow returns to the week with progress preserved.

The hero card

Dark, calm, single number

42 items, ~£38, a slim progress bar. It mirrors the dark "today" chapter on the week so the two screens feel like sisters. Quick actions (Share / PDF / Mark all) live in a single inset row beneath it.

Aisle as chapter

Each section is a small story

Produce, Protein, Pantry, Dairy, Frozen, Other. Each headed by a tinted icon and a count. Items show which days they're for ("Wed dinner · Sun lunch") so it never feels disconnected from the meals.

Already at home

A pantry-tag section at the bottom

Light striped panel with staples the user has marked permanent (olive oil, salt, soy sauce). They never appear in the main list — but they're visible, removable, and a quiet reminder.

Long-press item

Skip · Swap · Don't suggest

Same gesture as the recipe ingredient pills. Skipping removes from this week's basket; "Don't suggest" pushes it into You → Foods to skip permanently.

Shared · ingredient long-press

"Don’t suggest mushrooms"

Tap or long-press any ingredient pill in a recipe or week view. A small sheet pops up: Skip in future plans / Just this week / Cancel. The skip is added to You → Foods to skip and the week regenerates around it.

Shared · onboarding does the diet

No more food list page

Diet preference (omnivore / pescatarian / vegetarian / vegan) is captured in onboarding and edited from You. The 200-item food checklist disappears — replaced by lightweight in-context skips.

Shared · settings home for skips

You → Foods to skip

A clean list of every ingredient you’ve marked. Tap to remove. One source of truth. Survives re-rolls and week swaps.
