Making the food guide visible

Today the food guide is buried two taps deep in the “You” drawer. Below are four directions for promoting it — from light (a Today hero card) to bold (a dedicated bottom-nav tab). Variant B is the strongest balance of visibility + simplicity.

Variant A

Hero card on Today

Lightest touch. A featured card pinned near the top of Today.
