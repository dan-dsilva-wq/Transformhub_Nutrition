What's been turned up

The same weekly plan, dressed in the same vocabulary as the home redesign — Fraunces serif, glass, drift, gradient covers.

  • "This week." Fraunces with an italic accent — same move as "Light, morning." on home. A small floating mark sits next to it.
  • Today, elevated. The first day card becomes a dark-teal hero with a slow aurora drift and a shine sweep — same language as the streak ribbon on home.
  • Day stats inline. kcal · protein · fiber · meals as a frosted strip inside the hero — tabular numerals, no ring needed.
  • Meal pucks with covers. Each meal gets a deterministic gradient + emoji glyph. Already shipped on home — reused here for visual continuity.
  • Quiet day cards. Frosted glass with a mood-tinted bleed at the top. Day name (Fraunces) + date pill. Cook-time + kcal pills on the right.
  • Shopping, two ways. A green pill-CTA sits up top (when shop is ready), and the dark "Built from this week" CTA sits at the foot — both echo home's next-action card.
  • Week pill bar. Frosted, rounded — back/forward + 3/day chip + re-roll. Same surface treatment as home tiles.
