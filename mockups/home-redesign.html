What's new in the air here

Same data, same screen — turned up to feel alive without going theme-park.

  • A living calorie ring. Conic gradient with a soft blurred halo that drifts. The end-cap is a luminous bead, not a stub.
  • Time-of-day micro-mark. A small sun/moon next to "morning." that floats. Sets the tone in 200ms.
  • Macros that celebrate. Each puck shows %, grams, and a tiny check badge once you hit goal.
  • Water that ripples. The blue tile has a real water surface. Steps tile gets a sparkline of the day.
  • Streak ribbon. A dark teal panel with the three numbers people actually want to see — streak, weight delta, adherence.
  • Subtle aurora behind everything. A 16-second drift, blurred 28px. You see motion peripherally, never directly.
  • Display serif for headers, tabular sans for numbers. Fraunces handles "Light, morning." Inter handles 1,284. Numbers don't shift width as they climb.
