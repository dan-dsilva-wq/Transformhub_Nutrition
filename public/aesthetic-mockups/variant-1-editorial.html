II.ii · Wednesday · Dinner

Salmon & asparagus.

Twelve minutes from cold pan to plate. Crisp skin, soft middle, bright lemon.
Calories
438
Protein
42 g
Time
12 min
Serves
1
Ingredients
  • Salmon fillet, 160 g
  • Asparagus, 1 bunch
  • Olive oil, 1 tbsp
  • Lemon, half
  • Salt, pepper, dill
Method
  1. Pat salmon dry, salt the skin generously.
  2. Cold non-stick pan, skin down, medium heat — 6 minutes untouched.
  3. Toss asparagus with oil and salt, throw alongside for the last 4 minutes.
  4. Flip salmon, 90 seconds, off the heat.
  5. Squeeze lemon, scatter dill, eat.
