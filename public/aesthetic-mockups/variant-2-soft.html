Wednesday · Dinner · 12 min

Salmon & asparagus

438kcal
42 gprotein
12min
1serves

Ingredients

🐟 Salmon fillet 160 g
🌿 Asparagus 1 bunch
🫒 Olive oil 1 tbsp
🍋 Lemon ½

Method

  1. Pat salmon dry, salt the skin generously.
  2. Cold non-stick pan, skin down, medium heat — 6 minutes.
  3. Toss asparagus with oil and salt, throw alongside for the last 4.
  4. Flip salmon, 90 seconds, off the heat.
  5. Squeeze lemon, scatter dill, eat.
