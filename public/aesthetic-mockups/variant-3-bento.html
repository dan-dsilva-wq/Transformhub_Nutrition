Salmon & asparagus.

12 minutes from cold pan to plate.
438
kcal
42g
protein
12
min
1
serves
Ingredients
4 things
🐟 Salmon fillet 160 g
🌿 Asparagus 1 bunch
🫒 Olive oil 1 tbsp
🍋 Lemon ½
Method
5 steps
1
Pat salmon dry, salt the skin generously.
2
Cold non-stick pan, skin down, medium heat — 6 minutes untouched.
3
Toss asparagus with oil & salt, throw alongside for the last 4.
4
Flip salmon, 90 seconds, off the heat.
5
Squeeze lemon, scatter dill, eat.
