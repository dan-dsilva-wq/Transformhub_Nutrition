Pick a food guide direction.

Three takes on the same redesign. Same data, same palette — different priorities. Open each, then tell me which one (or which mash-up).

  • › D · Newest

    Two-tile hub

    Simple landing: 🥗 Your foods + 📅 Your week. Tap a tile to drill in. Week page has swap-meal, prev/next-week stepper for history + planning ahead, and a meals/day toggle. Built from your latest feedback.
  • › A · Shopping-first

    The grocery sheet

    Big, scannable list of foods you can eat with per-meal portions in grams + hand. Optimised for printing once and using all week. Meals collapsed below. Closest to "single page I take to the supermarket".
  • › B · Plate-first

    The cooking page

    Each meal slot gets a card with grams of P/C/F/Fi and example combos pulled from your approved list. Allowed foods are secondary. Ideal if you cook from the page rather than shop from it.
  • › C · Magazine

    The whole-week spread

    Editorial layout. Targets up top, allowed-foods grid in the middle, full 7-day plan as a calendar grid, deduplicated shopping list at the bottom. Densest, prints to ~2 pages of A4.
