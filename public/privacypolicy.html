Pace Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 April 2026

Pace is a nutrition and weight-loss companion for adults. This policy explains what data the app uses, why it uses it, and the choices you have.

Information you provide

Pace may store your account details, profile inputs, goals, food logs, meal photos, progress photos, weight entries, step counts, water logs, workout preferences, reminders, and coach messages. These are used to calculate targets, show your progress, generate meal estimates, and keep your data available across devices when you sign in.

Camera, photos, and barcode lookups

Camera access is used only when you choose to scan a barcode or add a meal/progress photo. Meal photos and notes may be sent to an AI provider to estimate nutrition. Barcode numbers may be sent to food database providers so Pace can return product nutrition data.

AI features

Coach messages, nutrition plan inputs, food preferences, meal notes, and selected photos may be sent to AI services to produce estimates, coaching replies, recipes, and lessons. Pace uses these responses for general wellness support only and does not provide medical diagnosis, medical nutrition therapy, pregnancy guidance, or eating disorder recovery care.

Health and activity data

On Android, Pace can read step and weight data from Health Connect when you choose to connect it from the in-app Integrations screen. The app requests only two read-only permissions — steps and weight — and uses them solely to display your daily step total on the Today screen and to populate your weight history when newer readings are available. Health Connect data is never sent to third parties, never used for advertising, and is not retained by Pace beyond the values shown in the app's normal weight log and step counter. You can revoke access at any time from the Health Connect app or from Android Settings → Apps → Pace → Permissions.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, Pace may schedule local notifications for meals, water, walks, or check-ins. You can turn notifications off in the app or in Android settings.

Sharing

Pace does not sell your personal data. Data may be processed by service providers that power authentication, database storage, AI features, food lookup, hosting, and app operations. Data may also be disclosed if required by law or to protect the service and users.

Control

You can edit or delete entries in the app. You can sign out to remove local session data from the device. To request account deletion or data export, use the contact method listed on the Google Play store listing.
